About Me

I’m a Data Scientist with 10+ years of experience across academia and industry, building AI systems that turn complexity into clarity. My core focus is on time series forecasting, generative AI, and real-time decision support — with applications in energy, mobility, and robotics.

I currently work at Alliander as a Senior Data Scientist, improving grid forecasting and risk modeling for energy infrastructure. I hold a PhD from TU Delft and CWI, where I specialized in machine learning and intelligent systems.

Professional Summary

My work blends deep technical skill with practical impact — from real-time forecasting models for the Dutch energy market to building my own AI assistant, Mr M, trained on my publications and projects. I actively contribute to open-source initiatives such as OpenSTEF, and have a track record of building systems that are both scalable and explainable.
